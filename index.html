Shop Score – Trust Score for E-commerce Customers

An AI-based customer reliability scoring system that helps marketplaces and sellers reduce fraud, optimize delivery options, and offer personalized experiences—all from a single 0–1000 score.

Check Shop Score

Problem Statement

Indian e-commerce platforms are facing major operational losses due to frequent order cancellations, delivery failures and unnecessary product returns.

No Customer Reliability System

There is no standardized method to evaluate how reliable a customer is before giving benefits like COD or discounts.

Losses for Platforms

Unpredictable buyer behavior leads to high logistics cost, return handling cost and operational losses.

Unfair Benefit Usage

Discounts, COD and delivery benefits are given without accountability, increasing misuse of services.

Reduced Trust

Platforms cannot identify trustworthy customers easily, reducing overall system efficiency and trust.

Our Solution

Shop Score introduces a trust-based scoring system that evaluates customers based on their behavior.

Customer Trust Score

Each user gets a Shop Score based on order history, cancellations, returns and payment behavior.

Identify Risk Users

System identifies high-risk and trustworthy customers to help platforms make better decisions.

Reward Good Customers

Responsible users receive better discounts, faster delivery and exclusive offers.

Control Risk

High-risk users may get restricted COD, advance payment options or limited benefits.

How It Works

01

Collect Data

Order history, cancellations, returns and payment data are collected.

02

Process Data

Data is cleaned, organized and prepared for analysis.

03

Analyze Behaviour

Customer order completion rate and reliability metrics are calculated.

04

Generate Score

System assigns a Shop Score between 0–1000 to each customer.

05

Apply Decisions

Benefits, delivery options and payment methods are decided based on score.

About Project

Shop Score is a trust-based scoring system designed to improve reliability and transparency in e-commerce platforms.

Smart Architecture

Admin & User Dashboards
